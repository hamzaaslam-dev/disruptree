Who we are

A studio for builders who refuse to wait.

disruptree is a distributed team of senior designers, engineers and AI specialists. We ship ambitious products fast — without the bloat of a traditional agency.

Our story

Built by operators, for operators.

We started disruptree after years of shipping inside fast-growth startups and enterprise R&D labs. We were tired of watching great ideas die in Jira tickets, committees and six-month timelines.

So we built the studio we wished existed: small, senior, opinionated. Every project led by someone who has scars, not slides. Every decision tied to business outcomes — shipped in weeks, not quarters.

What we stand for

Four principles, applied relentlessly.

01

Senior-only squads

Every engineer and designer on your project has 6+ years of experience. No juniors practicing on your product.

02

Outcomes over outputs

We measure ourselves in your revenue, retention and reduced cost — not hours logged or tickets closed.

03

Radical transparency

Shared Linear, shared Slack, weekly demos, open pricing. You always know what's happening and why.

04

Built to hand off

Clean code, full documentation, knowledge transfer. You keep the keys — we're your force multiplier, not your dependency.

05

Design & engineering as one

Designers live in the code, engineers live in Figma. The seams that slow teams down? We removed them.

06

AI-native from day one

We use AI to ship 3–5× faster, and weave it into the products we build so you compound the same advantage.
