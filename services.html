Our capabilities

Seven disciplines, one team.

From first-line of code to your first million users — we handle the whole journey under one roof. Pick a single service or bundle them into a full product launch.

01 — Web Development

Fast, conversion-driven, future-proof websites.

From marketing sites that rank & convert to complex SaaS products and internal tools — we build with React, Next.js, TypeScript and Tailwind, atop modern CMS layers like Sanity, Contentful or Payload.

  • Marketing & landing sites
  • SaaS platforms & dashboards
  • Headless CMS integrations
  • Progressive web apps (PWA)
  • Core Web Vitals optimisation
  • Internationalisation & SEO
02 — Mobile Development

Native-feel apps for iOS & Android.

Cross-platform speed with native polish. Flutter, React Native, Swift and Kotlin — shipped to the App Store and Play Store, hardened with CI/CD and crash-free reporting.

  • iOS & Android native
  • Flutter & React Native
  • App Store & Play submissions
  • Offline-first architecture
  • Push notifications & analytics
  • Performance & crash monitoring
03 — Automation & Ops

Run your business on autopilot.

We map your workflows, eliminate the busywork and connect your tools into one intelligent system. n8n, Make, Zapier, Pipedream — or custom pipelines engineered from scratch.

  • n8n, Make & Zapier pipelines
  • CRM & marketing automation
  • Data enrichment & scraping
  • Internal tools & Retool dashboards
  • Slack / Discord / WhatsApp bots
  • ETL & data warehousing
04 — AI & Machine Learning

Production AI, not demos.

We productionise models the messy world actually runs on — with evaluation, observability and guardrails. Hugging Face, PyTorch, TensorFlow, LangChain, LlamaIndex, OpenAI, Anthropic, open-source.

  • RAG & semantic search
  • Fine-tuning & custom models
  • Computer vision (YOLO, SAM)
  • NLP & document intelligence
  • Forecasting & recommendation
  • MLOps & evaluation pipelines
05 — Agentic AI

Autonomous agents that do real work.

Multi-agent systems that plan, reason and act across your tools. Sales agents that qualify leads, research agents that brief your team, ops agents that close tickets — all with memory, tools and guardrails.

  • Multi-agent orchestration
  • Tool-use & function calling
  • Long-term memory & context
  • Human-in-the-loop workflows
  • Voice agents (Retell, Vapi)
  • Evaluation & safety testing
06 — Graphic & Brand Design

Brand systems that punch above their weight.

Identity, design systems, motion, marketing assets and pixel-perfect UI — the visual layer that turns a product into a brand people recommend.

  • UI / UX design systems
  • Pitch decks & print

Frequently asked

Everything you want to ask before the first call.

How fast can we start?

Most projects kick off within one week. Once the scope is agreed, we slot in a senior squad (designer, engineer, PM) and hold a discovery sprint in the first 3–5 days.

Do you work fixed-price or retainer?

Both. Fixed-scope launches are priced per project; ongoing product work, AI agents and growth engineering run on monthly retainers starting at $6k.

Which tools & stacks do you use?

Next.js, React, TypeScript, Tailwind, Node, Python, Flutter, Swift, Postgres, Supabase, AWS, Vercel, OpenAI, Anthropic, n8n, LangChain and more. We'll pick the right one for your problem.

Who owns the code & IP?

You do. On day one, all code, design files, models and credentials are yours — transferred on delivery with zero lock-in.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes. Send yours before the first call or use ours. We've shipped under strict NDAs for enterprise & stealth startups.
